Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and booking process.

How far in advance should I book my safari?

We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance for peak season (June-October) and 1-3 months for other times of the year to ensure availability.

What is included in your safari packages?

Our safari packages typically include accommodation, park fees, game drives, professional guide, meals as specified, and transportation. Exact inclusions vary by package.

Do you offer airport transfers?

Yes, we provide reliable airport transfer services from all major airports in Tanzania. You can book this as part of your package or as a standalone service.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, credit cards, and mobile money payments. A deposit is required to confirm your booking, with the balance due before your travel date.

Can I customize a tour package?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating custom itineraries tailored to your interests, budget, and schedule. Contact us to discuss your specific requirements.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy varies depending on the service and timing. Generally, deposits are refundable up to a certain period before travel. Details are provided during booking.
